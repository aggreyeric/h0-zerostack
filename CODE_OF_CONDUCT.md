# Code of Conduct

We adopt the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in ZeroDeploy a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior:** empathy, respect, grace under pressure, constructive feedback, and accountability.

**Unacceptable:** sexualized language, trolling, personal/political attacks, doxxing, or other unprofessional conduct.

## Enforcement

Maintainers remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code. Repeated violations may result in a ban. Report issues privately to the maintainers. All reports are reviewed with discretion.

This code applies in all project spaces (GitHub, chat, docs, events).
